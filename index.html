Welcome to Osijek

Discover the beauty of the city on the Drava river

Osijek is a city located in eastern Croatia, he is laying on river Drava. It's known for its history and beautiful places. In Osijek you can enjoy in long walks along the Drava promenade.

Zoo and Kompa

You can cross the river with the bridge, which is in the middle of the promanade. Besides the bridge, you can also cross the Drava river by small ship called Kompa. You can drive with the Kompa only during the summer days and it is for free. When you croos the river you can find the Zoo Hotel and the zoo. In the zoo you can view a lots of animals and after that you can rest in the caffe bar. Caffe bar is owned by the Zoo hotel which is by the river.

Bikes and scooters

If you like riding a bike or a electric scooter you can rent it on many places in Osijek. Osijek is long city and it's covered with many bicycle roads.

Trams and shopping

And if you're not the sporty type and prefer something more relaxed, you can take a tram ride and see the whole city. And maybe visit the large shopping center called Portanova.
